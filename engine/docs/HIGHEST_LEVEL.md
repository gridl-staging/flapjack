# Flapjack Strategic Direction

**Last updated:** 2026-03-27
**Status:** Archived high-level positioning notes. Current launch priorities live in [`../docs2/1_STRATEGY/HIGHEST_PRIORITY.md`](../docs2/1_STRATEGY/HIGHEST_PRIORITY.md).

## Current North Star

Flapjack should be the best open-source path for teams that want Algolia-compatible search without handing their data to a hosted search vendor.

## Durable Product Direction

- **Algolia compatibility remains the primary product constraint.** Matching established client behavior is still the fastest path to trust and adoption.
- **Open-source launch quality matters.** The first-run experience, docs, and dashboard polish should make Flapjack feel complete rather than merely promising.
- **Single-tenant excellence comes first.** Multi-tenant and SaaS-oriented work remains downstream of a polished open-source release.
